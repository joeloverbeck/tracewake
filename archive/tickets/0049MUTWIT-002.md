## What to Change

### 1. Holder-mismatch and frontier-regression witnesses

Add tests for `actor_known_interval_delta`: (a) before/after contexts differing only in viewer actor → expect `ContextHolderMismatch`; (b) differing only in bound actor → expect `ContextHolderMismatch` (together these kill `|| → &&`); (c) `before.event_frontier() == after.event_frontier()` → expect a successful delta (NOT `FrontierRegression`), and `before > after` → expect `FrontierRegression` (kills `> → ==/>=`).

### 2. `source_key` accessor witness

Construct a `VerifiedActorKnownIntervalNotice` with a known `source_key` and assert `notice.source_key()` returns exactly that value (kills `""` and `"xyzzy"`). If the accessor participates in ordering at `projections.rs:~263`, optionally also assert the tie-break ordering of two notices with distinct keys.
